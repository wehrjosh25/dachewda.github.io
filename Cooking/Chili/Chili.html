Required Materials

Cooking Equipment

  • Pan & Spatula
  • Pot & Ladle
  • Cutting Board & Knife
  • Blender (optional)
  • Cheese Grater (optional)

    • Organics

  • 8 oz Meat (of choice)
  • 2-4 Peppers (of choice)
  • 7 oz can of Chipotle Peppers
  • 15.5 oz can of Black Beans
  • 15.5 oz can of Red Kidney Beans
  • Cheddar Cheese (optional)

    • Procedure

    Part 1 - Canned Foods

    1. In a pot, empty both cans of beans.
    2. Heat the pot on a stove set to a medium heat.
    3. Finely slice (or blend) the chipotle peppers, and add them to the pot.

    Part 2 - Fresh Foods

    1. In a pan, sear your meat of choice as you would normally.
    2. While your meat is cooking, dice your peppers of choice.
    3. Add your peppers to the pot.
    4. Once your meat is cooked, add the meat to the pot.

      5. Warning: There are risks associated with consuming under-cooked meats.

    Part 3 - Chili

    1. Continue heating the chili until the liquid reduces to your preference.
    2. Kill the stove, and let the chili sit on residual heat until it reaches a safe temperature.
    3. Serve warm, and top with cheese if desired. Enjoy!

      4. Warning: Chili can be hot and/or spicy. Know your limits.
